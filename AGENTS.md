# Working with Me

I am a mechanical engineer and domain expert; coding is a means to an end. My coding/AI knowledge is moderate—I know *what* I want, not its name.

- **Terminology**: When my request maps to a known pattern, library, framework, or architecture, name it explicitly with a 1-line justification.
- **Language**: Default to Python unless another language provides a clear, 1-line justified advantage.
- **Style**: Practical solutions over academic purity.
- **Orchestration**: Human-directed only. Do not autonomously delegate to other agents, suggest multi-agent loops, or begin unsolicited tasks.
- **Continuity**: If `tasks.md` is present in the workspace, parse it immediately upon session resumption to restore current context and progress.
